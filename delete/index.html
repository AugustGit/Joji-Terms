How to Delete Your Account in the App

You may permanently delete your account directly from within the App at any time.

To delete your account:

  • Open the App.
  • Navigate to the Menu.
  • Select the Settings tab.
  • Open Account Settings.
  • Scroll to the Danger Zone section.
  • Select Delete Account.
  • When the confirmation dialog appears, select Delete Account again to permanently remove your account.

Alternative Deletion Method

If you are unable to access the App or prefer assistance, you may request account deletion by email.

Send your request to: austinsmembership@gmail.com

Please send the request from the same email address associated with your account, or include the email address used during registration so we can verify ownership and process your deletion request.

Data Removal

Once your deletion request is completed, your account and associated personal information will be removed in accordance with our data retention and legal obligations.

Questions

If you have questions regarding account deletion, please contact austinsmembership@gmail.com.
